Principles of Responsible AI

Here are the key principles to ensure AI is developed and used responsibly.

Fairness

AI should be unbiased and treat all individuals and groups fairly.

Transparency

AI's decision-making processes should be clear and understandable.

Safety

AI systems should not pose harm to individuals, society, or the environment.

Privacy

AI should respect and protect user data and privacy.

Accountability

Humans should take responsibility for AI's actions and decisions.
